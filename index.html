Vacations in Spain

1600$ - 5 Days

"Immerse yourself in the magic of Spain with a tour that combines the vibrancy of Madrid, the enchanting beauty of Porto, and the historic antiquity of Lisbon, for an experience rich in culture and beauty."

A trip to Spain is a magical adventure that takes you on a tour of its picturesque cities and historical landmarks. The journey begins in Madrid, the vibrant capital, where you can explore royal palaces and famous art museums. Then, head to Porto, the charming Portuguese city, to enjoy the beauty of the Douro River and its romantic alleys. The adventure is completed in Lisbon, a city that combines tradition and modernity, where ancient castles and streets filled with live music will captivate your soul. This trip promises you an experience rich in culture, art, and delicious tastes that will remain in your memory forever.

Day 1: Madrid

-Arrive in Madrid. Start your day with a visit to the Royal Palace, one of Europe's largest and most impressive palaces. Walk through Plaza Mayor, a historic square filled with vibrant cafes and street performers.
-Explore the Prado Museum, home to one of the finest collections of European art. Enjoy a leisurely stroll through El Retiro Park, Madrid's most famous park.

Day 2: Madrid to Porto

-Take a quick flight or train to Porto. Check into your hotel. Then explore the historic Ribeira district, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the famous Livraria Lello bookstore, known for its stunning architecture.
-Enjoy a traditional Portuguese meal at a riverside restaurant. Try the local specialty, Francesinha, a hearty sandwich covered in melted cheese and a rich tomato sauce.

Day 3: Porto

-Visit the Porto Cathedral and climb the Clerigos Tower for a panoramic view of the city. Walk across the Dom Luís I Bridge for a scenic view of the Douro River.
-Take a guided tour of one of Porto's famous port wine cellars in Vila Nova de Gaia. Learn about the wine-making process and enjoy a tasting session.

Day 4: Porto to Lisbon

-Begin with a visit to Belém Tower, a historic fortress that has become a symbol of Lisbon. Explore the Jerónimos Monastery, a stunning example of Manueline architecture.

Day 5: Lisbon

-Visit the Lisbon Oceanarium, one of the largest aquariums in Europe. Explore the nearby Parque das Nações, a modern district with beautiful waterfront views
-Take the historic tram 28 to see the city's famous landmarks, including the Lisbon Cathedral and the São Jorge Castle.
